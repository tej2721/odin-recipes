Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made by layering wide pasta sheets with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy bechamel sauce or ricotta cheese, and plenty of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. It is then baked until the cheese melts and turns golden brown, creating a delicious combination of flavors and textures.

Lasagna is loved around the world because it is hearty, filling, and easy to customize. Some people prefer the traditional beef version, while others enjoy vegetarian or chicken lasagna. It is often served at family gatherings and special occasions, making it a comforting meal that brings people together.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Beef
  • Pasta
  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the onion
  2. Peel the garlic
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Cook the beef
  5. Take the pasta
  6. Pour oil
  7. Add pepper
  8. Add sauce
