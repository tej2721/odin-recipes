Description

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). It is thin, crispy, and golden brown on the outside while remaining soft inside. Dosa is commonly served with coconut chutney and sambar, making it a delicious and nutritious meal.

Dosa can be enjoyed in many varieties, such as plain dosa, masala dosa, cheese dosa, and onion dosa. It is a favorite breakfast and dinner dish because it is light, healthy, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Green chillies
  • Ghee
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Pour water
  3. Add oil
  4. Make dosa
