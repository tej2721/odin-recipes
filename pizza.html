Description

Pizza is a famous Italian dish made with a soft, round dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It is baked in an oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted, creating a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Pizza comes in many styles and flavors, including Margherita, Pepperoni, Veggie, and BBQ Chicken. It is enjoyed all over the world and is a popular choice for parties, family dinners, and casual meals.
